About Me

This is what I do.

Hey! I'm Mansha 😊 — someone who enjoys coding 👩🏻‍💻 as much as I enjoy a good movie, a great meal, or a random late-night thought that turns into a blog post. This space isn’t limited to one thing — it’s where I explore everything that sparks my curiosity, whether it's debugging a tricky function, binge-watching a series, or trying out a new café in town.

I started this blog as a way to document, express, and maybe even entertain. Some days I might write about tech tips or tools I’m loving, and on others, it might be about the best paneer tikka I've had this month or a plot twist I didn’t see coming. Think of it as a digital journal that doesn’t like sticking to one theme.

If you're someone who appreciates a mix of bytes and bites, screen time and screenplays, you're in the right place. Thanks for stopping by — and I hope you find something here that makes you smile, think, or just take a break from the scroll ✨
